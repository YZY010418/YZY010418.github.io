Research Projects
======
**Design cyclic peptide binders**

Built million-scale cyclic-peptide-receptor structure datasets by mimicking intra-protein interfaces and screening cyclic-compatible conformations. CPSea1 uses residue distance criteria, and CPSea2 uses terminal amide pose criteria to support model scaling for cyclic peptide binder design.

**Design mirror-image peptide binders**

Introduced axial-vector and pseudo-scalar features to explicitly encode molecular chirality, helping generative models match training-set chirality, generalize across chirality for hetero-chiral design, and control chirality patterns.

**Assist protein folding by glycosylation**

Showed that glycosylation can prevent aggregation during folding, enabling the synthesis of multiple proteins previously considered difficult to fold.

**Triple-split inteins for total synthesis of ultra-large proteins**

Discovered a split intein retaining high splicing efficiency when split into three segments, helping overcome concentration and denaturant limits in total synthesis of proteins larger than 100 kDa.
